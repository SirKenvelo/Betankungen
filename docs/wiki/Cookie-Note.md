# Cookie Note
**Stand:** 2026-04-19

This page is a small personal side note. It stays intentionally brief and
does not replace technical documentation.

But it does something equally important: it keeps a bit of heart inside the
project.

## Editorial Role

Cookie is part of the project's day-to-day reality.

While the code focuses on structure, correctness and long-term maintainability,
this note gently reminds us that not everything is about schemas, flags and
CLI arguments.

Sometimes it's about:
- a short break
- a walk outside
- a ball that absolutely must be chased
- or a tiny dog that thinks she can take on the entire world

This page exists to preserve that perspective - lightweight, warm, and
intentionally separate from the technical core.
