# Code of Conduct
**Stand:** 2026-03-30

## Purpose

Betankungen is maintained as a public technical repository. Collaboration
should stay respectful, concrete, and useful for the project and its users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Keep technical disagreement factual, specific, and solution-oriented.
- Assume good intent first, then clarify with concrete evidence.
- Protect privacy, security, and project integrity when sharing examples,
  logs, or screenshots.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or demeaning language.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary behavior.
- Publishing personal data, secrets, or sensitive operational details without
  clear permission.
- Repeated spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Bad-faith bug reports or review comments without actionable substance.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository spaces and project-related
interactions, including issues, pull requests, reviews, wiki content, and
discussion around repository work.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, close, or remove content that violates
these rules. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent
exclusion from further participation.

## Reporting

If you observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer through a private
GitHub channel instead of escalating publicly. If the report also includes a
security concern, follow the process documented in `SECURITY.md`.
